SIR M VISHVESHVARAYA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

ABOUT US

Sir MVIT is a well-known institution in Bangalore, established in 1986 by the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust. Situated on a large 133-acre campus near the Kempegowda International Airport in Yelahanka, Bangalore.It is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) in Belagavi, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade and NBA.
